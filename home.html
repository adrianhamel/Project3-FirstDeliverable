Adventures Page

I like to ride around Downtown Chicago at night on Divvy Scooters and E-Bikes a lot.

I would go from Grant park all the way back to IIT for fun.

I would even go to the planetarium at night on Lakefront Trail to enjoy the Chicago Skyline and stars.

Since my dorm room faces the lake, I would always wake up early in the morning to view the beautiful sunrise.

During finals week, me and a group of friends took a trip down to St. Louis by train.

We spent a long time getting there, but we ate great food and got to see many sights and landmarks.
